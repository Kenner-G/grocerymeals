About GroceryMeals

GroceryMeals is an online platform that gathers the best grocery prices and matches them with recipes you can make at home!

Best Prices

GroceryMeals automatically gets the best grocery prices from your favorite stores and displays them for your picking!

Amazing recipes

GroceryMeals can match amazing and delicious recipes to whatever groceries that you would like to purchase. We can also provide you with recipes to make that include multiple specified ingredients.

Completely Free!

We have made GroceryMeals completely free, because finding the best deals that you can cook at home shouldn't come with any extra fees!

About GroceryMeals

GroceryMeals is a small tech startup started by Calix Huang, based in the East Bay Area, CA in the United States. This platform was built with HTML/CSS/Javascript, Python/Django, and other web frameworks such as jQuery and Bootstrap. GroceryMeals is hosted on Heroku/GitHub, using AWS technologies for storage and the DNS nameservers belong to Namecheap.
